About Us

We are the Panther Coders!

As eighth grade students, this competition posed as a wonderful opportunity to use what we are learning about code to create our own unique website! We all love to help out our community, but cannot do much as kids. This website allows us to bring attention to important organizations near you. Throughout ths project, everyone on our team contributed greatly and sacrificed time, so we could do something that we all love: code! Most of us became friends through this competition, and we combined our strengths to form a great team!
