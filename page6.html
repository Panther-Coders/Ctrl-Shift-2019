One Laptop Per Child (OLPC) Organization


OLPC's mission is to empower the poorest children in the world to succeed through education. They work all around the world in countries like, Madagascar, Kenya, India, Peru, and many more. OLPC has teams who analyze the economic and social state of the communities they work in to create a personalized program to fit the needs of the children in the area. However, their main focusses are program design implementation and logistics, teacher training, maintenance and technical support, monitoring and evaluation, volunteer program, community support and development


For more information on this organization visit the one laptop per child website.
